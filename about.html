Building Excellence Since 2010

Prasad Construction, Founded by Suresh Ghavghave, Prasad Construction has been a trusted name in the construction industry for over 15 years, delivering outstanding residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our reputation is built on quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and on-time delivery. We don’t just build structures — we create lasting spaces that inspire trust and stand the test of time.
